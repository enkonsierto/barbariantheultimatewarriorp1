B99E: 5-digit decimal divider table.
Five 2-byte little-endian powers of 10 used by print_dec_number to decompose a value into individual decimal digits: 10000, 1000, 100, 10, 1.
divder_table B99E DEFW $2710
B9A0 DEFW $03E8
B9A2 DEFW $0064
2-digit display entry point. entry_point_0 calls print_dec_number_0 with IX=47524 to print the round timer using only the last two divisors (10 and 1).
B9A4 DEFB $0A,$00
B9A6 DEFB $01,$00
