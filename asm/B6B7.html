B6B7: Score award frame table.
3-byte entries: (attacker_frame_id, score_low, score_high).
If IY+8 matches attacker_frame_id, adds DE score delta to p1_score (P1) or R47431 (P2), depending on IYl: 89=P1 barbarian, 98=P2 barbarian.
Searched by hit_processor via find_hit_section. Terminated by sentinel 0x80.
Called after the hit indicator phase in hit_processor. Same search pattern as hit_indicator_frame_table.
