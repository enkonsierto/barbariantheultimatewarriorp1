99C7: Energy decrement, bar display and KO detection.
Used by the routines at pos_energy_reset, post_fight_setup and collision_solver.
Called by collision_solver after a hit connects with a non-zero knockback type.
Decrements the energy value stored in the low nibble of IY+7 (range 0-13).
If energy underflows (was 0, DEC wraps to 255), triggers KO sequence: sets movement 27 (death) on the loser and movement 36 (victory pose) on the winner, then forces the energy display to show the empty bar.
SELF-MODIFYING TARGET: the instruction at p1_energy_gate is patched from RET Z to NOP by one_player_mode and two_player_mode on game start. In the unpatched binary it prevents P1 from ever losing energy (demo/attract mode protection).
INPUT: IY = defender state block (p1_controller_type P1 or p2_controller_type P2).
OUTPUT: None. DESTROYS: AF, BC, DE, HL.
