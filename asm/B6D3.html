B6D3: Pending combat sound index.
Stores the index of the combat sound triggered this frame.
Values: 1=sound_kick, 2=sound_tackle, 3=sound_slash, 4=sound_head_cut, 5=sound_guard_1, 6=sound_guard_2.
Written by collision_solver (bits 4-7 of hit byte 2, shifted right 4 via RRCA x4).
Read and cleared by hit_processor (hit processor and sound dispatcher).
When 0, hit_processor returns immediately without any processing. Index 5-6 skip the visual indicator and score logic, going directly to the sound dispatch table.
pending_sound_index B6D3 DEFB $00 Combat sound index. 0=none, 1=Kick, 2=Tackle, 3=Slash, 4=Head Cut, 5-6=Guard.
