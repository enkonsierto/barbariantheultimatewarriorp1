B9A8: Snake animation pointer table.
Table of 14 active 16-bit pointers to snake sprite frames in snake_sprite_0, indexed by the energy bar draw phase (bits 7-4 of IY+7). Forms a ping-pong cycle through 4 frames: 1-1-1-2-3-3-4-4-3-3-2-2-1-1
Used by update_snake_frame_id via solve_pointer to resolve the frame pointer for each phase.
