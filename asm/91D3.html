91D3: Direct copy tile renderer.
Used by the routine at tile_render_engine.
Copies tile data directly to the framebuffer without masking: 3 bytes (24 pixels) wide, B scanlines high.
Framebuffer advances 32 bytes per row; source DE stays in single 256-byte page.
INPUT: B scanline count (21 full tile or sub-tile height). DE tile source address (low byte only advanced). HL framebuffer destination.
OUTPUT: None. DESTROYS: AF,BC,DE,HL.
direct_copy_tile_renderer 91D3 LD B,$15 Sets B=21 for full tile height (entry from R36910).
This entry point is used by the routine at comp_tile_dispatcher.
direct_copy_tile_renderer_0 91D5 PUSH HL Saves start-of-scanline HL.
91D6 LD A,(DE) Copies source byte 0 to framebuffer.
91D7 LD (HL),A
91D8 INC E Points to the next byte and framebuffer address.
91D9 INC L
91DA LD A,(DE) Saves start-of-scanline HL.
91DB LD (HL),A Copies source byte 1 to framebuffer.
91DC INC E
91DD INC L Points to the next byte and framebuffer address.
91DE LD A,(DE)
91DF LD (HL),A Saves start-of-scanline HL.
91E0 INC E Advances past 3rd source byte.
91E1 POP HL Restores start-of-scanline HL.
91E2 PUSH DE Advances HL 32 bytes to next framebuffer row.
91E3 LD DE,$0020
91E6 ADD HL,DE
91E7 POP DE Recovers tile address.
91E8 DJNZ direct_copy_tile_renderer_0 Repeats B times for full height.
91EA RET Returns.
