B717: Final boss spell tile sequence (unused).
Tile index sequence alternating IDs 18 and 22, resolved against sprite bank base other_sprites. Belongs to the final boss projectile spell attack, present in another version of the game. The final boss does not appear in this build and this sequence is not referenced by any runtime code.
Dead data retained from another game version. Format identical to p1_head_tile_seq and p2_head_tile_seq (tile IDs + sentinel 255).
