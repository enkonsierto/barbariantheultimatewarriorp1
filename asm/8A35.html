8A35: Game Over, Game Reset.
Used by the routines at entry_point, end_of_fight_dispatcher and post_drain_wait.
Resets game state and returns to the menu sequence at entry_point_0.
IY+7 is a packed byte: bits 7-4 hold the snake animation draw phase (0=idle, 1-13=active cycle), bits 3-0 hold the current energy value (0-13). Writing 0x10 arms the snake animation for one step before reset_p1_state_block and reset_p2_state_block restore energy to 13.
game_reset 8A35 LD A,$10 Sets energy to 16 (0x10) in both player state blocks, priming the animation. Energy is restored to 13 by reset_p1_state_block at 89EB and reset_p2_state_block at 89EE.
