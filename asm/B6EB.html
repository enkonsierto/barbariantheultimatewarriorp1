B6EB: Energy bar UDG indices.
7-entry table of packed UDG indices for 3-segment energy bar (0-6 segments).
Indexed by current energy / 2 (IY+7 >> 1). Each byte packs 3 x 2-bit segment types (0=empty, 1=low, 2=med, 3=full).
Index = energy >> 1 (IY+7). Used by 99DF to select UDGs for HUD blit at VRAM 16416(P1)/16445(P2).
energy_bar_attrs B6EB DEFB $00 0 segs: all empty
B6EC DEFB $01 1 seg: medium circle, empty, empty
B6ED DEFB $02 2 segs: full circle, empty, empty
B6EE DEFB $06 3 segs: full circle, medium circle, empty
B6EF DEFB $0A 4 segs: full circle, full circle, empty
B6F0 DEFB $1A 5 segs: full circle, full circle, medium circle
B6F1 DEFB $2A 6 segs: full circle, full circle, full circle
