9C9C: 3-frame cycle counter advance.
Used by the routine at entry_point.
Advances frame_counter_hit by 1 each call.
Active frame (counter=0) runs tackle_side_swap (pre-HALT) and hit_detection (hit detection).
INPUT: None. OUTPUT: None. DESTROYS: AF.
frame_counter_hit_inc 9C9C LD A,($B973) Load current counter from frame_counter_hit.
9C9F INC A Increment the counter.
9CA0 LD ($B973),A Store updated counter back to frame_counter_hit.
9CA3 CP $02 Counter reached 2 - reset it to 0.
9CA5 RET NZ Not yet - return with counter at 1.
9CA6 XOR A Reset counter to 0.
9CA7 LD ($B973),A Store reset value back to frame_counter_hit.
9CAA RET Return.
