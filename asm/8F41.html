8F41: Menu fade-out transition.
Used by the routines at main_menu, one_player_mode, key_assign and two_player_mode.
Gradually darkens the menu by decrementing the ink attribute of three rows over 7 steps with 112ms delay between each step, producing a fade-to-black effect. Then self-modifies waits_112ms to skip its internal wait, clears the screen instantly, and restores waits_112ms to its original value before returning.
Called from main_menu when transitioning away from the menu screen.
DESTROYS AF, BC, HL.
menu_fade_out 8F41 LD B,$07 7-step fade loop counter (3 rows each loop).
menu_fade_out_0 8F43 PUSH BC Saves the loop counter.
8F44 LD HL,$58E2 Points to the first tile of the game window.
8F47 INC HL Adds 1.
8F48 LD BC,$1A0F B=26 (Columns), C=3 (Rows).
8F4B CALL fade_ink Decrements ink of three menu rows.
8F4E CALL waits_112ms Waits 112ms.
8F51 POP BC Restores the loop counter.
8F52 DJNZ menu_fade_out_0 Repeats until counter reaches 0.
8F54 LD A,$C9 Self-modifying code: patches waits_112ms with RET so the wait is skipped.
8F56 LD ($9BA6),A
8F59 CALL sweep_transition Clears the screen without waiting.
8F5C LD A,$01 Restores waits_112ms to its original value.
8F5E LD ($9BA6),A
8F61 RET
