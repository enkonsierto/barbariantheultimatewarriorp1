93D3: Post-drain wait and end-of-round jump.
Called by p1_death_score_update or p2_death_score_update in a 2 Players game, once the score-drain loop terminates (timer_flag=0).
Waits 2 x 112 ms (~224 ms pause) then jumps to game_reset to clear combat state and back to the menu.
post_drain_wait 93D3 CALL waits_112ms Wait 224 ms.
93D6 CALL waits_112ms
93D9 JP game_reset Jump to game_reset to clear state and back to the menu.
