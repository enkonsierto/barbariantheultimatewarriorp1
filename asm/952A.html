952A: Deactivate flying head: exited screen.
Called from head_animator when the head column reaches 0, 1 or 29 (screen edges).
Writes 0 to IX+1 (head_state_flag): head is now fully inactive.
INPUT: None. OUTPUT: None. DESTROYS: AF.
deactivate_head 952A XOR A Head has left the screen - clear active flag.
952B LD (IX+$01),A Set head_state_flag to 0: head deactivated.
952E RET
