8A7A: Aligns the hit indicator screen address to a tile boundary.
Used by the routine at entry_point.
hit_processor stores the hit indicator address with a +2 column offset. This routine strips it each frame by applying AND F8h to the low byte of hit_indicator_screen_addr, ready for the next draw call.
DESTROYS: A, HL
align_hit_indicator 8A7A LD HL,($B6D6) HL = hit_indicator_screen_addr, load the low byte into A.
8A7D LD A,(HL)
8A7E AND $F8 AND F8h: clear bits 2-0, strip the +2 column offset.
8A80 LD (HL),A Write aligned low byte back to hit_indicator_screen_addr.
8A81 RET Return.
