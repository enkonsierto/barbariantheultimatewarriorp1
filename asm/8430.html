8430: Music player.
Used by the routine at main_menu.
Title screen music player. Resets the melody and bass to the start, plays the music in loop and returns when a key is pressed.
play_music 8430 LD HL,$8539 Saves melody_data (the main melody address) in position melody_ptr.
8433 LD ($844B),HL
8436 LD HL,$876F Saves bass_data (the bass melody address) in position bass_ptr.
8439 LD ($844F),HL
843C DI Disable interrupts.
play_music_0 843D CALL music_engine Plays Music.
8440 CALL $028E Keyboard ROM Routine. (Returns the key pressed in register E).
8443 INC E If E = 255 no key was pressed and returns.
8444 JR Z,play_music_0
8446 EI Enable interrupts.
8447 RET Returns.
