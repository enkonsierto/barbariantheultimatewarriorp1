How to end the game

2 PLAYERS MODE

Have fun with a friend!

This part is basically made thinking in to combat with another human player, so there's no completition. The 2 players game was removed from part 2 (although the routines didn't were removed).

1 PLAYER MODE

Defeat the opponents endlessly

If you play in 1 player mode, this part of the game is endless and you will fight one combat after another with opponents although the fight counter will stay in 7. Unfortunately you will never face Drax.

As soon as you start the game, knock your opponent into the corner by rolling into hin and then constanly knock him over. After every two rolls, kick him down until dead.

On the earlier fight levels of the game, the warriors are more susceptible to the (now legendary) 'spin around and chop his head off' move.

So, use this as an opportunity to beat hell out of the guy with either stomach or head slashes (200pts each) until he's right down low on his energy, then chop his head off for maximum points value - a move that nets 500pts, if I'm not wrong. This is a great way to bolster the points up for a high score, but it won't work on the higher opponents.
