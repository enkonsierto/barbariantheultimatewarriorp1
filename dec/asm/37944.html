37944: Initialise flying head state block.
Called by decap_setup when the decapitation hit frame 17 is detected and no head is already in flight.
Writes the direction flag and framebuffer position directly into head_state and head_screen_addr, then falls through to R37950 to set the active state, trajectory pointer and tile sequence pointer.
INPUT: A = direction flag (0=right, 1=left). HL = initial framebuffer address. DE = trajectory data pointer (normal_traj_data). BC = tile sequence pointer (p1_head_tile_seq or p2_head_tile_seq).
OUTPUT: None. DESTROYS: A.
head_init 37944 LD (46809),A Write direction flag to head_state.
37947 LD (46815),HL Write framebuffer address to head_screen_addr (16-bit store).
Second entry point used by goblin_kicks when the goblin kicks the severed head. Re-activates the head with a new trajectory and tile source without touching direction head_state or current position head_screen_addr; the head is already on screen and just needs a new bounce arc.
INPUT: DE = kicked_traj_data kicked-head bounce trajectory. BC = tile sequence pointer (p1_head_tile_seqor p2_head_tile_seq).
head_reactivate 37950 LD A,1 A = 1, head active and bouncing.
37952 LD (46810),A head_state_flag = 1: mark head as active, re-enables R37997 movement logic.
37955 LD (46813),DE head_traj_ptr = DE: trajectory data pointer.
37959 LD (46811),BC head_frame_seq = BC: tile frame sequence pointer.
37963 RET Return.
