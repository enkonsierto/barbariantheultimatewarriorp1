38790: Single scanline clear for snake-edge rows (26 central tiles).
Used by the routine at clear_snake_row.
Setups a counter of 26 loops and deletes the pointed scanline of a complete row. Used by clear_snake_row.
INPUT: HL = VRAM address of the first tile of the row.
OUTPUT: HL = restored to the entry value. DESTROYS A, B, DE.
