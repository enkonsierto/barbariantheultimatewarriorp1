47798: AI profile data blocks.
22 blocks of 8 bytes each. Each byte is a movement_block_id (0-22) that selects an 8-byte row in ai_movement_table. The column within the profile is chosen by second_ai_state_table[fighter_distance] (secondary AI table, range 0-7).
Profiles with low block IDs (0-6) are passive/defensive.
Profiles with high block IDs (14-22) are aggressive.
Most profiles end with [4,8]: block 4 and block 8 are identical (both = [1,5,1,1,1,1,1,1], biased toward attack-5 and forward-1).
