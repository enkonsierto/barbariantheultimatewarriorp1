36673: Menu fade-out transition.
Used by the routines at main_menu, one_player_mode, key_assign and two_player_mode.
Gradually darkens the menu by decrementing the ink attribute of three rows over 7 steps with 112ms delay between each step, producing a fade-to-black effect. Then self-modifies waits_112ms to skip its internal wait, clears the screen instantly, and restores waits_112ms to its original value before returning.
Called from main_menu when transitioning away from the menu screen.
DESTROYS AF, BC, HL.
menu_fade_out 36673 LD B,7 7-step fade loop counter (3 rows each loop).
menu_fade_out_0 36675 PUSH BC Saves the loop counter.
36676 LD HL,22754 Points to the first tile of the game window.
36679 INC HL Adds 1.
36680 LD BC,6671 B=26 (Columns), C=3 (Rows).
36683 CALL fade_ink Decrements ink of three menu rows.
36686 CALL waits_112ms Waits 112ms.
36689 POP BC Restores the loop counter.
36690 DJNZ menu_fade_out_0 Repeats until counter reaches 0.
36692 LD A,201 Self-modifying code: patches waits_112ms with RET so the wait is skipped.
36694 LD (39846),A
36697 CALL sweep_transition Clears the screen without waiting.
36700 LD A,1 Restores waits_112ms to its original value.
36702 LD (39846),A
36705 RET
