39897: Emits one square-wave pulse.
Used by the routine at beeper_engine.
Plays one complete HIGH-LOW cycle for the given period value. Called by beeper_engine once per non-zero period byte in the sound data table.
INPUT: A = period value (higher = lower pitch). OUTPUT: None. DESTROYS: AF, BC.
beeper_emit 39897 LD B,A B = period value.
39898 PUSH BC Preserve BC across HIGH phase.
39899 CALL beep_high_phase HIGH phase: B-iteration delay loop then speaker HIGH via beep_high_phase.
39902 POP BC Restore BC.
39903 JP beep_low_phase LOW phase: B-iteration delay loop then speaker LOW via beep_low_phase.
