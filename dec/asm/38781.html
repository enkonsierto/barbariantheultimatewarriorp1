38781: Derive attribute high byte from a VRAM pixel address.
Converts the high byte of a VRAM pixel address to the corresponding attribute high byte. Extracts the screen third (0-2) from H and combines it with the attribute area base (0x58) to produce 0x58, 0x59 or 0x5A. L is not touched, so the caller can form the full attribute address directly from A and L.
INPUT: HL = VRAM pixel address. OUTPUT: A attribute high byte (0x58, 0x59 or 0x5A). DESTROYS: A.
get_attr_address 38781 LD A,H Shift H right by 3 and mask to bits 1-0 to isolate the screen third (0-2).
