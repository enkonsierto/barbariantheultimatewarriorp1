46806: Hit indicator screen address.
2-byte VRAM address where the hit indicator glyph will be drawn.
Computed by hit_processor from the attacker X tile position (IY+3), row offset, and mirror flag (IY+2 bit 0), then stored here before calling draw_character.
Written and consumed within the same call to hit_processor.
Skipped if the computed column falls on boundary tiles (0, 1, 30, 31).
hit_indicator_screen_addr 46806 DEFB 0,0 Calculated VRAM address for hit indicator glyph. Written by hit_processor.
