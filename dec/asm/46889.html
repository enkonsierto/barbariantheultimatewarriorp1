Normal trajectory data.
Table of 2-byte step pairs (control, magnitude) for the severed head after a normal decapitation. One pair is consumed per frame by head_animator via the trajectory pointer at IX4,5 (head_traj_ptr).
Control byte: bit 0 set (value 1) moves the head up by magnitude scanlines; bit 1 set (value 2) moves it down; value 0 holds position. Magnitude is the vertical delta in scanlines applied that frame.
The head rises 9 scanlines over 2 frames (6+3), holds one frame at the peak, then falls with increasing speed (2, 4, 6, 8, 10, 12, 14 scanlines per frame), bounces slightly upward (4+2), and settles to the ground.
Loaded into IX4,5 (head_traj_ptr) by head_init at the moment of decapitation. 18 step pairs, 36 bytes total.

Goblin-kick head trajectory.
Table of 2-byte step pairs (control, magnitude) for the severed head after the goblin kick (goblin_kicks). Replaces the active trajectory pointer at IX4,5 (head_traj_ptr) via head_reactivate.
Same format as normal_traj_data: control byte 1 moves up, 2 moves down, 0 holds. Magnitude is the vertical delta in scanlines applied that frame.
The head rises 9 scanlines over 2 frames (6+3), holds one frame, falls 9 scanlines (6+3), holds, bounces up 3 scanlines, holds, settles down 3 scanlines, then rests.
Loaded into IX4,5 (head_traj_ptr) by goblin_kicks via head_reactivate after the goblin kick. 17 step pairs, 34 bytes total.
