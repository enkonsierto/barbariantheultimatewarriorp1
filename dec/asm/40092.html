40092: 3-frame cycle counter advance.
Used by the routine at entry_point.
Advances frame_counter_hit by 1 each call.
Active frame (counter=0) runs tackle_side_swap (pre-HALT) and hit_detection (hit detection).
INPUT: None. OUTPUT: None. DESTROYS: AF.
frame_counter_hit_inc 40092 LD A,(47475) Load current counter from frame_counter_hit.
40095 INC A Increment the counter.
40096 LD (47475),A Store updated counter back to frame_counter_hit.
40099 CP 2 Counter reached 2 - reset it to 0.
40101 RET NZ Not yet - return with counter at 1.
40102 XOR A Reset counter to 0.
40103 LD (47475),A Store reset value back to frame_counter_hit.
40106 RET Return.
