38180: Mark head landed.
Mark flying head as landed: trajectory exhausted, enter draw-only phase.
Reached when head_animator detects the end-of-trajectory sentinel in the tile animation sequence.
Sets head_state_flag to 255, switching head_animator from movement mode to draw-only mode each frame.
Also unblocks goblin spawn: goblin_entry waits for head_state_flag = 255 before the goblin enters the arena.
INPUT: None. OUTPUT: None. DESTROYS: AF.
mark_head_landed 38180 LD A,255 End of bounce trajectory - mark head as landed.
38182 LD (IX+1),A Set head_state_flag to 255 - head is now in draw-only landed state.
38185 RET Return.
