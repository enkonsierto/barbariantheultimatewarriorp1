36822: Character position and energy reset.
Used by the routines at reset_p1_state_block and reset_p2_state_block.
Writes A to IY+3 (X tile position), applies movement 28 (idle stand) via set_char_moves, sets IY+7 to 13 (full energy), then tail-calls energy_dec to immediately refresh the on-screen energy bar.
INPUT: A =starting X tile position (0=left for P1, 23=right for P2). IY = character state block (p1_controller_type or p2_controller_type).
OUTPUT: None. DESTROYS AF, BC.
pos_energy_reset 36822 LD (IY+3),A Write A to IY+3: place character at starting X tile.
36825 LD A,28 Apply movement 28 (enter the arena) to IY via set_char_moves.
36827 CALL set_char_moves
36830 LD A,13 Set IY+7 to 13: full energy.
36832 LD (IY+7),A
36835 JP energy_dec Call energy_dec to redraw the energy bar.
