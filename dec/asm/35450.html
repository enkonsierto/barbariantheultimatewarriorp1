35450: Aligns the hit indicator screen address to a tile boundary.
Used by the routine at entry_point.
hit_processor stores the hit indicator address with a +2 column offset. This routine strips it each frame by applying AND F8h to the low byte of hit_indicator_screen_addr, ready for the next draw call.
DESTROYS: A, HL
align_hit_indicator 35450 LD HL,(46806) HL = hit_indicator_screen_addr, load the low byte into A.
35453 LD A,(HL)
35454 AND 248 AND F8h: clear bits 2-0, strip the +2 column offset.
35456 LD (HL),A Write aligned low byte back to hit_indicator_screen_addr.
35457 RET Return.
