38137: Flying head ground shadow renderer.
Used by the routine at head_animator.
Draws a ground shadow for the flying head each frame. Calculates the shadow VRAM address from the current head column (head_screen_addr) and the ground row, then calls tile_render_engine to blit the shadow tile (40576).
INPUT: None. OUTPUT: None. DESTROYS: AF, DE, HL.
shadow_head 38137 LD A,(46434) Guard: return if p2_controller_type is zero (never true during normal gameplay).
38140 OR A
38141 RET Z
38142 LD DE,40576 Load head shadow tile source address (40576).
38145 LD A,(46815) Read current head column from framebuffer address (head_screen_addr).
38148 AND 31 Build VRAM column byte: got the equivalent address in the ground.
38150 OR 224
38152 LD H,101 Complete VRAM address in HL.
38154 LD L,A
38155 JP tile_render_engine Tail-call tile_render_engine to render the shadow tile.
