35426: Score display updater.
Redraws the 5-digit score for one player to the HUD.
Called every frame from the game loop and once at initialisation via entry_point.
Entry at paint_score targets Player 1; entry at paint_score_0 targets Player 2.
INPUT: None. OUTPUT: None. DESTROYS: AF, BC, DE, HL, IX and shadow registers.
paint_score 35426 LD HL,16553 Sets HL to the Player 1 score HUD tile address.
35429 LD DE,(47429) Loads the Player 1 score value address into DE.
35433 JR paint_score_1 Jumps to the shared print path.
This entry point updates the Player 2 score.
Used by the routines at entry_point and p1_death_score_update.
paint_score_0 35435 LD HL,16562 First tile address of the Player 2 score HUD.
35438 LD DE,(47431) VRAM destination for Player 2 score R47431.
paint_score_1 35442 PUSH AF Preserves A.
35443 LD B,5 Sets digit count to 5.
35445 CALL print_dec_number Converts and draws the score via print_dec_number.
35448 POP AF Restores A.
35449 RET Return.
