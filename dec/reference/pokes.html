Infinite Energy
POKE 36220,0
LD (39371),A > LD (203),A
(tries to write in the ROM zone = does nothing).
POKE 36516,0
LD (39371),A > LD (203),A
(tries to write in the ROM zone = does nothing).
POKE 39371,200
If this poke was applied later after setting the controls, (when p1_energy_gate was patched to NOP), this poke restores RET Z, so P1 never decrements its energy.
Those pokes avoid the self-modify patch that enables the P1 depletion.
