9786: Single scanline clear for snake-edge rows (26 central tiles).
Used by the routine at clear_snake_row.
Setups a counter of 26 loops and deletes the pointed scanline of a complete row. Used by clear_snake_row.
INPUT: HL = VRAM address of the first tile of the row.
OUTPUT: HL = restored to the entry value. DESTROYS A, B, DE.
single_scanline_clear 9786 PUSH HL Preserve the entry VRAM address.
9787 LD B,$1A B=26, skipping the leftmost snake column.
9789 INC HL Advance past the leftmost snake column.
978A JR clear_scanline_ink_attr_0 Jump into the shared scanline-clear loop at clear_scanline_ink_attr.
